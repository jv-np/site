---
title: why your dashboard is too padded
date: 2026-04-12
readingMin: 6
tags: [design, ui]
---

most dashboards waste 60% of the viewport on negative space that does not earn its keep.
when every card breathes the same way, **nothing breathes**. density is information.

## the rule

you get *one* generous gap budget per surface. spend it on the structural seams,
not on internal padding. a tight grid of tight cards reads faster than a sparse
grid of airy cards — every time.
