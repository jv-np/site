most people prompt AI like they're talking to an intern: open-ended, conversational,
trusting it to fill in blanks. **macro coding** flips that. you write a tight,
unambiguous *macro* — a structured intent — and the AI expands it into code.

- the macro is the source of truth, not the output
- you re-run macros, you don't re-prompt them
- the artifact is reproducible because the macro is
