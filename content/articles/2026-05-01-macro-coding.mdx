instead of prompting the AI to do everything for you, why you don't code in a higher level and let the AI fill the gaps?

## precision
normal AI coding, "vibe coding" is full of traps regarding the precision of what you mean, it's also prone to gradual destruction as you ask the agents to build more and more stuff

macro-coding is an approach that tries to take the other side, you write the code as you want it to be:
* cool and comfortable APIs
* predictable results
* comments explaining what's desirable

the AI builds the implementation details, it fills in the gaps from your code from the errors and comments

---

when you think about it, is this so different from using a library?
the AI is doing the job of the library author, you are consuming the code in the most desirable way for you, and you avoid the supply chain risk of shipping yet another dependency.
